> Introduction

Hi there,

My public name is Kenji Mouri, or 毛利 研二 in Japanese. In China, due to my public name, my friends call me 毛利, or Mouri in English. In some cases, my friends also call me Kuriko Mou, or 毛 栗子 in Japanese.

I am passionate about building efficient software with concise implementations and minimal third-party dependencies, and love creating open-source projects and sometimes making contributions for other's. I am also a Microsoft MVP and a PCBeta moderator.
